CHALLENGE

As a fast-moving startup, Govini’s design resources and platform were managed haphazardly as priorities shifted. We reinvented the wheel and missed inconsistencies too often. Our data-heavy use cases had bloated the functions of our platform, causing it to look outdated and complex. Shared knowledge was stored in the brains of individual contributors. When they went on vacation or left the company, our whole team suffered.

SOLUTION

I lead a year-long effort with our design and engineering teams to create a design system for Govini’s Decision Science Platform. I created 43-page site map, built 400+ component states in Figma, completed a platform reskin, integrated design tokens into our codebase, and stood up a documentation page for the company.
