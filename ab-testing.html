CHALLENGE

I worked with The Washington Post’s subscription tools team on two internal marketing tools: Wall Builder and Offer Builder. Wall Builder is an editor for our paywalls, softwalls, and other acquisition touchpoints, while Offer Builder is an editor for offer pages that describe our product plans and pricing. Our marketing team is constantly running tests to determine what aspects convert users best: a change in color, a catchy headline, or maybe a new CTA. These experiments are critical to forming an optimized data-driven user journey, but take up too much product, design, and engineering resources.

SOLUTION

I led the design of A/B testing features for Wall Builder and Offer Builder (launched in July 2022 and February 2023, respectively). These solutions allow marketing to independently design, setup, and run experiments, speeding up experimentation and increasing the bandwidth of our teams.
